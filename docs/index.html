About the Project

Everyday is a visual novel in which the player meets and befriends a cast of characters over the course of several in-game weeks. The story will be told through a series of scenes in which the player will get to know the characters. Some of these scenes can be experienced in a player-chosen order, and almost all of them will have dialogue choices for the player to make. There may also be multiple endings that the player can get, depending on certain choices they make.


Everyday takes place over the course of _ in-game weeks. Each week, the player can choose which characters to interact with and when. Each day, the player will be presented with a map screen and can see where each of the six characters are located. Traveling to the location of a character will initiate an event with them. Through these events, the player will get to know each character better and witness their character development, as well as learn about their relationships with each other. Once per week the entire team will meet at the office/studio to check in on development progress. It will be primarily during these events that the main story advances.
