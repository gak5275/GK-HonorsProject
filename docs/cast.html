Cast

Player Character

The player character can be named by the player, but their default name is Jackie. Their gender can also be chosen by the player. Shortly before the story begins, they applied for a job as a video game playtester at publisher Nomina Games and got accepted. The game begins with them being introduced to their new workplace and colleagues.


Ren Kuang

Age: 24

Likes: friendly competition, computers
Dislikes: greediness, gambling, social media

Ren is the programmer of ___. He comes from a wealthy family and was homeschooled. As a result, he has met most of his friends online. He was formerly a member of an amateur esports team, but recently quit. He enjoys MMOs, MOBAs, and other cooperative multiplayer games, and he prefers to play games on PC.

Relationships: Ren gets along well with Olive due to their shared extroversion.

Themes of Ren's story: Accountability


Amelia Ulrich

Age: 30

Likes: her younger siblings, staying informed
Dislikes: dishonesty, people who talk too much

Amelia is a newly hired marketing intern who has been assigned by Nomina to market ___. She often questions the morality of her own work, believing most marketing strategies to be dishonest and manipulative. She is fairly introverted, but skilled at reading social cues and not afraid to call people out on their mistakes. Her only experience with video games is watching her brother and sister play them from time to time. She spends much of her time taking care of her younger siblings, as her parents are frequently traveling for work. Amelia is the first member of her family to go to college, which is something that she is proud of and does not take for granted.

Relationships: Amelia is constantly at odds with Yosef due to their conflicting personalities.

Themes of Amelia's story: Honesty, Family


Yosef Peters

Age: 33

Likes: being valued, meeting new people, motorcycles
Dislikes: party-poopers, pretentiousness

Yosef is a newly hired financial expert who has been assigned by Nomina to manage the finances for Dev Team 51. He is the oldest member of the team and enjoys acting as a mentor figure toward his colleagues, despite being new to the company. Born in Eastern Europe, he moved to the United States after graduating from college. He knows almost nothing about video games, only that they are a rapidly growing industry.

Relationships: Despite his best efforts to be friends with everybody, Yosef is constantly at odds with Amelia due to their conflicting personalities. Because of his intense sociability, some members of the team like Donovan and Noel find him somewhat intimidating.

Themes of Yosef's story: Self-worth


Donovan Farley

Age: 25

Likes: writing, video games, night time
Dislikes: talking, hypocrisy, unwanted opinions

Donovan is the main writer of ___. He is very socially withdrawn and would rather express his thoughts through writing than speaking. He has yet to really open up to any of his teammates. He plays video games the most out of everyone on the team, with a preference for narrative-driven games on home consoles. While Donovan does try to get input and feedback from his teammates, most of the dialogue in _ comes from his own mind.

Relationships:

Themes of Donovan's story: Longing


Olive Emani

Likes: music, dancing, plants
Dislikes: school

Olive is the composer for the soundtrack of ___. She has a passion for music of all kinds. However, that passion tends to be in the form of listening to music rather than making it herself. Some might call her lazy, but she would strongly disagree. Her passion for music does not always translate to talent in creating it. She enjoys cozy, laid-back games with simplistic gameplay, and most often plays them on her phone.

Relationships: Olive has been friends with Noel since high school. The two of them are currently roommates and applied to work at Nomina together. She also gets along well with Ren since they are both outgoing.

Themes of Olive's story: Acknowledging limitations


Noel Richards

Likes: cute animals, cartoons
Dislikes: confrontation, trying new things

Noel is the artist for ___. She is immensely shy, but secretly wishes for praise and approval. While she is humble about it, she is very academically gifted and once wished to become a teacher. She is currently attending art school while doing commissions for Nomina Games. She does not have much experience with video games, but is interested in them.

Relationships: Noel and Olive have been friends since high school. The two of them are currently roommates and applied to work at Nomina together.

Themes of Noel's story: Ambition, Self-improvement, The Weight of Expectations
