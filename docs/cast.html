Cast

Player Character - The Newbie

The player character can be named by the player, but their default name is Jackie. Their gender can also be chosen by the player. Shortly before the story begins, they applied for a job as a video game playtester at publisher Nomina Games and got accepted. When the game starts, they are moving to Elysa, the city in which Nomina is located. Their new apartment happens to be in the same building as the company’s offices, just a few floors higher. The team they join is referred to as Development Team 51, and besides G is comprised of six other members.


G - The Unseen Leader

G is the mysterious leader of Dev Team 51 and the original visionary for Common Grounds. Due to dubious circumstances, they are managing the team remotely from another undisclosed location. G is the one who conducted the player’s job interview, though it was done completely over the internet and neither one saw the other’s face.


Ren Kang - The Lead Programmer

Likes: Friendly competition, computers

Dislikes: Greediness, gambling, social media


Ren is the programmer of Common Grounds. He comes from a wealthy family and was homeschooled. As a result, he has met most of his friends online. He was formerly a member of an amateur esports team, but recently quit. He enjoys MMOs, MOBAs, and other cooperative multiplayer games, and he prefers to play games on PC.


Relationships:


Themes of Ren's story: Accountability


Amelia Ulrich - The Marketing Manager

Likes: Her younger siblings, staying informed

Dislikes: Dishonesty, people who talk too much


Amelia is a newly hired marketing intern who has been assigned by Nomina to market Common Grounds. She often questions the morality of her own work, believing most marketing strategies to be dishonest and manipulative. She is fairly introverted, but skilled at reading social cues and not afraid to call people out on their mistakes. Her only experience with video games is watching her brother and sister play them from time to time. She spends much of her time taking care of her younger siblings, as her parents are frequently traveling for work.


Relationships: Amelia is constantly at odds with Yosef due to their conflicting personalities.


Themes of Amelia's story: Honesty, Family


Yosef Peters - The Finance Manager

Likes: Being valued, meeting new people, motorcycles

Dislikes: Party-poopers, fancy words


Yosef is a newly hired financial expert who has been assigned by Nomina to manage the finances for Dev Team 51. He is the oldest member of the team and enjoys acting as a mentor figure toward his colleagues, despite being new to the company. Born in Eastern Europe, he moved to the United States after graduating from college. He knows almost nothing about video games, only that they are a rapidly growing industry.


Relationships: Despite his best efforts to be friends with everybody, Yosef is constantly at odds with Amelia due to their conflicting personalities. Because of his intense sociability, some members of the team like Donovan and Noel find him somewhat intimidating.


Themes of Yosef's story: Self-worth


Donovan Farley - The Writer

Likes: Writing, video games, night time

Dislikes: Talking, hypocrisy, unwanted opinions


Donovan is the main writer of Common Grounds. He is very socially withdrawn and would rather express his thoughts through writing than speaking. He has yet to really open up to any of his teammates. He plays video games the most out of everyone on the team, with a preference for narrative-driven games on home consoles. Despite his introversion, he has apparently known G the longest out of anyone on the team.


Relationships:


Themes of Donovan's story: Longing


Olive Estrada - The Composer

Likes: Social media, plants

Dislikes: school


Olive is the composer for the soundtrack of Common Grounds. She has a passion for music of all kinds. However, that passion tends to be in the form of listening to music rather than making it herself. Some might call her lazy, but she would strongly disagree. Her passion for music does not always translate to talent in creating it. She enjoys cozy, laid-back games with simplistic gameplay, and most often plays them on her phone.


Relationships: Olive has been friends with Noel since high school. The two of them are currently roommates and applied to work at Nomina together. She also gets along well with Ren since they are both outgoing.


Themes of Olive's story: Acknowledging limitations


Noel Richards - The Artist

Likes: Cute animals, cartoons

Dislikes: Critics, confrontation, trying new things


Noel is the artist for Common Grounds. She is immensely shy, but secretly wishes for praise and approval. While she is humble about it, she is very academically gifted and once wished to become a teacher. She is currently attending art school while doing commissions for Nomina Games. She does not have much experience with video games, but is interested in them.


Relationships: Noel and Olive have been friends since high school. The two of them are currently roommates and applied to work at Nomina together.


Themes of Noel's story: Ambition, Self-improvement
