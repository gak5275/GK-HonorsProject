About

My project for the Schreyer Honors Program will be a visual novel titled Everyday. Everyday is a visual novel in which the player meets and befriends a cast of characters over the course of several in-game weeks. The story will be told through a series of scenes in which the player will get to know the characters. Some of these scenes can be experienced in a player-chosen order, and almost all of them will have dialogue choices for the player to make. The main story will also explore the struggles and challenges that can arise when working in the video game industry. Not all characters will have completely good intentions or sound judgement. There may be multiple endings that the player can get, depending on certain choices they make.

From Wikipedia: "A visual novel, often abbreviated as VN, is a form of digital semi-interactive fiction. Visual novels are often associated with and used in the medium of video games, but are not always labeled as such themselves.They combine a textual narrative with static or animated illustrations and a varying degree of interactivity".
