Thesis Proposal

Making a Game About Making Games

My objective for my project is to create an informative and entertaining story-driven game that conveys the challenges of working in the modern video game industry through its narrative and characters. I began envisioning the game's premise last fall and have been adding more ideas about the story and characters as I come up with them. I will begin writing for the game this summer, and begin developing the game in Unity in the fall. The written component of my thesis will detail the creation process of my game and the research that I am conducting.

The intended outcome of my project is for myself and anyone who plays my game to gain a better understanding of what it is like to work in the video game industry, including the struggles that those who make games face on a regular basis. I will publish the game on the website itch.io.

I will earn honors credits from an independent study (DIGIT 496) and the DIGIT senior project (DIGIT 494).

I will meet with my thesis advisor once each week during my senior year.

My thesis will satisfy six honors credits as well as my senior project in the DIGIT major.
